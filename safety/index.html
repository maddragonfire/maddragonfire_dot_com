Know the risk before you light.

Fire performance is inherently dangerous.

Igniting, handling, spinning, wearing, maintaining, fueling, transporting, or performing with fire props can cause severe burns, other bodily injury, death, fire, and damage to property or the environment. No design, warning, instruction, training, or safety practice can eliminate every risk.

Responsible use

Mad Dragon Fire products are specialized equipment, not toys. Keep them away from children. They are intended only for trained, sober, competent adults who understand the equipment, the fuel being used, the performance environment, and the risks involved.

Before each use, inspect the entire prop and all consumable, fastened, welded, and wear components. Do not use damaged, loose, excessively worn, improperly assembled, or modified equipment. Follow all product-specific instructions and warnings. Use appropriate protective measures, competent safety personnel, fire-suppression resources, clearances, and emergency planning.

The user is responsible for determining whether use is safe and lawful in the specific location and circumstances, including compliance with permits, fire codes, venue rules, land restrictions, and other applicable requirements. If you are not prepared to manage and accept the risks, do not ignite or use the prop.
